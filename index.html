Project Erostribe Presents:

#CircusAndCode

"Innovation at the Speed of Art."


#CircusAndCode is an attempt to spark technological innovation through the exploration of circus arts.


#CircusAndCode is project that uses circus arts to teach coding and agile methodology by creating art that encourages the exploration of technology and computer science.

Our current focus "The Quest for Pink Huntober" is an multimedia project with the goal of creating a stage production where we join our main character (Corderosa Amada Kugla) on an other-worldly experience that inspires the love of technology and the passion for JavaScript.

Together the #CircusAndCode community will learn JavaScript, use ChatGPT and MidJourney to create the plot and art for the project and explore the sights and sounds of the new world our character finds herself a part of.
